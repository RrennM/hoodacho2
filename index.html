Hi, I'm Dave.

Living in the Pacific Northwest means we see our fair share of wet, cold weather. Personally, I'm a fan of layers, but at a certain point, I start to feel like the kid from that christmas movie who is so bundled, he can't put his arms down! So, I set out to find a fix for this and what I came up with was the Hoodacho. Hoodacho's are a cozier, more versatile cowl-like alternative to other headwear that you can wear with any of your favorite garments, whether hooded or not, to keep you warmer and dry.
